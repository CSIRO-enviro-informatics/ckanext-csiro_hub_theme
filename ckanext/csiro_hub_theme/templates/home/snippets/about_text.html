The CSIRO Digital Asset Registry has been setup as part of the CSIRO Land and Water Digital Asset Management Committee by the DAMbusters project.

The registry is a customisation of CKAN
.

Documentation and support

Please refer to the Digital Asset Registry User Guide here for documentation to get started and tips.

The Digital Asset Registry is part of a broader strategic digital asset management plan. Refer to Digital Asset Management Strategy on Confluence for more info.

This registry is operated as a "research operations" grade platform. The registry team endeavours to address any issues and/or service disruptions during normal business hours (between 9am-5pm AEST Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays). Weekly backups will be put in place using IM&T system backup arrangements.

If you have questions, our FAQ page may assist, or feel free to contact us at lw-dam-support@csiro.au.

Comments and suggestions to DAMC are welcome on the L&W Digital Asset Management Suggestions Confluence page.
