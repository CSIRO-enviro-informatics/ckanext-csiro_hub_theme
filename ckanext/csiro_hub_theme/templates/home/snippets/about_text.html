The CSIRO Digital Asset Registry has been setup as part of the CSIRO Land and Water Digital Asset Management Committee by the Dambusters project (formerly the OzNome for Land & Water project).

The registry is a customisation of CKAN

Documentation and support

Please refer to the Digital Asset Registry User Guide here for documentation to get started and tips.

If you have questions, our FAQ page may assist, or feel free to contact us at lw-dam-support@csiro.au.
