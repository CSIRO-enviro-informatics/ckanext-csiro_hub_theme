The CSIRO Land and Water Digital Asset Registry allows CSIRO users to register digital assets for audit purposes and discovery by CSIRO internal staff.

What is a 'digital asset'?

Digital Assets are self-contained digital objects that are of value to an individual or an organisation, such as datasets, software, web sites, web services and other digital platforms.

What is this registry for?

This registry serves as a platform for registering digital assets that are not considered appropriate for 'external-release' or not owned by CSIRO but used by research projects (e.g. licensed data).

This registry facilitates discovery of other relevant CSIRO digital assets previously registered on other systems - internal (e.g. CSIRO DAP), or external (e.g. Dryad, data.gov.au).

What about assets that are suitable for external release?

Please consider depositing 'external-release' ready digital assets or if you have repository requirements for your data in the CSIRO's Data Access Portal (DAP) at http://data.csiro.au/dap.
